"Nocturne" from Serenade for Tenor, Horn, and Strings (Benjamin Britten) is an aria with text from Alfred, Lord Tennyson's "The Princess" about an all girl's school. Here, the narrator describes the premises of the school, where his beloved (who has shunned the world of men) resides and sings of his love.
Accompanist: Robby Baine
